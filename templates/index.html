Why does mood affect our level of productivity?

Often people experience difficulty during activities when faced with a bad mood, in general this is often experienced by teenagers. To overcome this, it is necessary to make efforts to control the mood to stay fine. One of them is by doing several activities that can improve your mood on the sidelines.
